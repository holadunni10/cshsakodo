🏫 About Our School

Shaping Futures in
Ibeju-Lekki

Community Senior High School, Akodo-Orofun is a government secondary school committed to providing quality education to students across Ibeju-Lekki, Lagos State. We pride ourselves on academic excellence, strong moral values, and the all-round development of every student we serve.

✉️ Principal's Message

Welcome to Our
School Community

On behalf of our dedicated staff and vibrant student community, I warmly welcome you to Community Senior High School, Akodo-Orofun. Our school is not just an institution — it is a family committed to nurturing every child's potential. We believe that every student has unique talents, and our role is to discover and develop those gifts through academic rigour, discipline, and character building.

We invite parents, guardians, and the broader community to partner with us in this noble mission of shaping the leaders of tomorrow. Together, we will build a brighter future for Ibeju-Lekki and Nigeria at large.

— Mr. Awoniran J.A., Principal
